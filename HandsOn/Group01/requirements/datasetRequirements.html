Dataset number 1

  • (R1)Datasets are in one of the selected smart city domains Colegios Públicos de Madrid.
  • (R2)Data are available as a CSV file.
  • (R3)Data have an open license, so they can be published: Las presentes condiciones generales permiten la reutilización de los documentos y datos sometidos a ellas para fines comerciales y no comerciales. Aviso Legal.
  • (R4) Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities:
    This dataset will be linked to other knowledge graphs such us Wikidata or DBpedia through features like:
    • In DBpedia: School , for example: Hogwarts.
    • In Wikidata: Location , for example: Madrid.

  • (R5)The data set has an pdf explaining the structure of the data. Estructura Conjunto de Datos.
  • (R6)The data comes from two different sources, this one and another described below.
